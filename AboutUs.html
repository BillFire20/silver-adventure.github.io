Our Idea

An out of the world AI Wardrobe that is user-friendly, makes choosing different outfits more convenient just by activating it with the user’s voice or touch screen.
They can listen to their favourite songs, watch videos and look at the weather forecast while choosing their preferred outfit with the AR(Augmented Reality) camera feature.


Our goal is to spread the latest fashion trend with our very own AI wardrobe. This new technology will revolutionise the wardrobe industry with opening room for expansions such as advertisement and algorithmically chosen clothes that better suit the user.
